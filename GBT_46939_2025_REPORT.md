## 1. Document Overview | 文档概览

**Full Title 全称**:
- 中文: 中医体质分类与判定
- English: Classification and Determination of Constitution of Traditional Chinese Medicine

**Standard Number 编号**: GB/T 46939—2025

**Issued 发布**: 2025-12-31
**Effective 实施**: 2026-04-01

**Issuing Bodies 发布机构**:
- State Administration for Market Regulation (国家市场监督管理总局)
- Standardization Administration of China (国家标准化管理委员会)

**Proposed by 提出**: National Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine (国家中医药管理局)

**Managed by 归口**: National Technical Committee on Traditional Chinese Medicine Standardization (SAC/TC478) (全国中医标准化技术委员会)

**Lead Drafting Institutions 主要起草单位**: Beijing University of Chinese Medicine (北京中医药大学), China Association of Chinese Medicine (中华中医药学会), Guangdong Provincial Hospital of Chinese Medicine (广东省中医院), plus 17 other medical universities and hospitals.

**Lead Author 主要起草人**: Wang Qi (王琦) — the founding scholar of TCM constitution theory.

**Significance 重要意义**:
This is an **upgrade from the 2009 industry standard** (ZYYXH/T 157—2009, issued by the China Association of Chinese Medicine) to a **full national standard (GB/T)**. The 2009 version has been applied in over **570 million person-times** across grassroots medical institutions and promoted in **1,734 Class II+ TCM hospitals** nationwide. It has been incorporated into **20+ national-level policy documents**. This 2025 national standard unifies and raises the bar for quality and consistency of TCM constitution assessment across clinical, public health, chronic disease management, and wellness industries.

## 2. Key Terminology | 关键术语

### 2.1 Constitution of Traditional Chinese Medicine | 中医体质

> **Chinese 原文**: 人体生命过程中，在先天禀赋和后天获得的基础上所形成的形态结构、生理功能、心理状态和适应能力方面综合的、相对稳定的固有特质；是人类在生长、发育过程中所形成的与自然、社会环境相适应的人体个体特征。

**English Translation**:
A comprehensive, relatively stable inherent quality formed during the process of human life, based on both innate endowment and postnatal acquisition, encompassing morphological structure, physiological function, psychological state, and adaptive capacity. It represents the individual physical characteristics formed during human growth and development in adaptation to the natural and social environment.

### 2.2 Constitution Classification | 体质分类

> **Chinese 原文**: 根据人群中的个体各自不同的表现，按照一定的标准，采用一定的方法，通过整理、分析、归纳而进行全面系统的分类，分成若干类型。

**English Translation**:
A comprehensive and systematic classification into distinct types, based on the varying individual manifestations within a population, conducted through organizing, analyzing, and synthesizing data according to established standards and methods.

### 2.3 Constitution Differentiation | 体质辨识

> **Chinese 原文**: 以人的体质为认知对象，从体质状态及不同体质分类的特性，把握其健康与疾病的整体要素与个体差异，从而为制定防治原则，选择相应的治疗、预防和养生方法奠定基础。

**English Translation**:
Taking human constitution as the object of cognition, grasping the holistic factors and individual differences in health and disease from the perspective of constitutional states and characteristics of different constitution types, thereby laying the foundation for formulating principles of prevention and treatment and selecting corresponding therapeutic, preventive, and wellness approaches.

### 2.4 Biased Constitution | 偏颇体质

> **Chinese 原文**: 9种基本中医体质类型中，除平和质外的其他体质类型。

**English Translation**:
Among the 9 basic TCM constitution types, all types other than the Balanced type (Ping-He). These represent constitutional tendencies that deviate from the ideal balanced state.

## 4. Scoring System | 评分体系

### 4.1 Method | 计分方法

Each question uses a **5-level Likert scale**:

| Score | Chinese | English |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | 没有（根本不）| Not at all |
| 2 | 很少（有一点）| Rarely (slightly) |
| 3 | 有时（有些）| Sometimes (somewhat) |
| 4 | 经常（相当）| Often (considerably) |
| 5 | 总是（非常）| Always (very much) |

### 4.2 Conversion Formula | 转化公式

```
Raw Score (原始分) = Sum of all item scores for that constitution
Converted Score (转化分) = [(Raw Score - Number of Items) / (Number of Items × 4)] × 100
```

This normalizes the score to a **0–100 scale**, where:
- Minimum possible = 0 (all items scored 1)
- Maximum possible = 100 (all items scored 5)

### 4.3 Determination Criteria | 判定标准

**Balanced Constitution (平和质)**:

| Condition | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Converted score ≥ 60 **AND** all other 8 types < 30 | **Yes (是)** |
| Converted score ≥ 60 **AND** all other 8 types < 40 | **Basically Yes (基本是)** |
| Does not meet above conditions | **No (否)** |

**Biased Constitutions (偏颇体质)** (the other 8 types):

| Condition | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Converted score ≥ 40 | **Yes (是)** |
| Converted score 30–39 | **Tendency (倾向是)** |
| Converted score < 30 | **No (否)** |

**Key insight**: A person can have **multiple biased constitutions simultaneously**. For example, someone can be both Qi-Deficiency and Yang-Deficiency. This is critical for accurate wellness guidance.

### Deduplication Summary | 去重说明

The standard defines 30 total items across 9 scales. Three items are shared:

| Shared Item | Appears In | Scoring |
|-------------|-----------|---------|

### 8.3 Key Difference from Current Implementation

Current system uses **weighted scoring** (each answer option has custom weights across constitutions). The national standard uses a **direct assignment** system (each question belongs to exactly one constitution scale, scored 1-5). This is simpler and more authoritative.

```
Current: answer → custom weight → multiple constitutions affected
Standard: answer → belongs to one scale → direct 1-5 score → formula
```

Exception: the 3 shared questions are scored into 2 scales each (one normal, one reverse).
